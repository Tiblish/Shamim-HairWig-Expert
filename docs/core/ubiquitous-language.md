# Ubiquitous Language

This document defines the shared vocabulary for all business, domain, and system-level concepts used across the Shamim Hair Stylist (SHS) Non-Surgical Hair Restoration application.

## 1. Core Domain Concepts

### Hair Patch / Hair Replacement System
* **Definition**: A premium, non-surgical hair system made of ethically sourced natural human hair configured on a breathable base (e.g., lace or skin base) to restore hair density.
* **Avoided Terms**: Wig, Toupee, Hair Extension.
* **Where Used**: Product Catalog, Service details, Before/After showcases, Custom fittings.

### Bonding / Bonding System
* **Definition**: The medical-grade attachment method used to secure a Hair Patch to the client's scalp skin. Can include liquid adhesives (bonding) or double-sided tape (retaping), customized for sport-proof, gym-proof, and waterproof wear.
* **Avoided Terms**: Gluing, Pasting.
* **Where Used**: Service configurations, Maintenance guides, FAQs.

### Lace Front / Lace Base
* **Definition**: A premium base type made of ultra-breathable, invisible lace mesh that offers an undetectable front hairline blending seamlessly with the scalp.
* **Avoided Terms**: Mesh, Net.
* **Where Used**: Product types, Pricing matrix, Before/After showcases.

### Micro-Scalp Scan / Scalp Consultation
* **Definition**: A professional microscopic profiling and assessment of the client's scalp health, skin type, and surrounding hair density to customize the base material and hair patch.
* **Avoided Terms**: Checkup, Scalp Exam.
* **Where Used**: Booking flow, Services Overview, CTAs.

### Stylist / Specialist
* **Definition**: A certified hair restoration professional at SHS trained to design, custom cut, style, blend, and maintain non-surgical hair systems.
* **Avoided Terms**: Beautician, Barber.
* **Where Used**: Staff listing, Appointment details, User dashboard.

### Transformation
* **Definition**: A verified client case showing the before-state (hair thinning/balding) and after-state (fully styled hair patch system), displayed to prove natural integration.
* **Avoided Terms**: Makeover, Haircut.
* **Where Used**: Hero Carousel, Social Proof Section, Before/After spot, Gallery page.

### Appointment
* **Definition**: A scheduled in-clinic session booked by a Client for a specific Hair Patch installation, Scalp Consultation, or maintenance service.
* **Avoided Terms**: Booking, Reservation.
* **Where Used**: Booking flow, User Dashboard, Clinic Admin panel.
